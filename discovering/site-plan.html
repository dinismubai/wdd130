Overview

Purpose

Think about a time you traveled for a vocation and you felt like you could enjoy more of that trip if you had a little more information about the place you were visiting. How could that have affected your experience? This website is designed especially with that intent. I want to help you know what to look for in the place you are visiting even before you are there and get the best out of your vocation so, come travel with me.

Audience

This website is intended for all the people who wish to have a pleasant holiday and like to know a little more about the places they are going to visit in this case, explore and discover before they get there. This site is meant to help tourists and other travel enthusiasts about the beauties and wonders of Mozambique.

Content

Home page

Traveling is one of the best ways to relax and enjoy the best nature has to offer. Meet Mozambique. A country of beauty and amazing landscape. The people there are very welcoming and they will surely make you feel at home. Give it a try, go visit Mozambique and it is a promise, you will never forget it. Mozambique is located on the South side of Africa and it is divided into 10 provinces. Each of the provinces has something unique and special about them. From the amazing sunrises and sunsets to beautiful sightseeing. We are here to help you have the best traveling experience ever informing you about everything wonderful you will find there.

Images for the Home page

[Page 2]

How do you feel about exploring and discovering new things? This is the proposal that we bring here in this wonderful adventure. To be connected with mother nature make us feel like we are part of something great and we are. When we are out there, contemplating all the beauty and wonders, we feel at peace and our minds get clear. Como enjoys these feelings here in Mozambique. If you are fond of mountain climbing, wild walks, natural sightseeing, and taking pictures, this is the place you want to go. Come have unforgettable moments in Mount Binga which is the highest mountain in Mozambique and visit the mountain that cries, yes, it cries and to make it more interesting it does look like a persons head.

Do you like wildlife? Well, this is the place to visit the famous big five. From the Lion, Buffalo, Elephant, Rhino, and the Leopard to all other Savana animals you will be amazed to be able to see them all in a wonderful safari. This is the house of the wildlife. This is where they have been living for a long time ago. Come and see for yourself, the Lion king awaits. Have you ever heard of the famous Black Mamba? Well, here is where it occurs more. Come get frightened by its majesty and tenacity, but do not worry, you will be wee guided and oriented at all times to make sure that your experience is the best possible.
