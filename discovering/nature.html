NATURE

How do you feel about exploring and discovering new things? This is the proposal that we bring here in this wonderful adventure. To be connected with mother nature make us feel like we are part of something great and, we are. When we are out there, contemplating all the beauty and wonders, we feel at peace and our minds get clear. Como enjoys these feelings here in Mozambique. If you are fond of mountain climbing, wild walks, natural sightseeing, and taking pictures, this is the place you want to go.

Come have unforgettable moments on Mount Binga which is the highest mountain in Mozambique and visit the mountain that cries, yes, it cries and to make it more interesting it does look like a person's head. Don't miss out. The beauty of Nature waits for you. Come and explore.
